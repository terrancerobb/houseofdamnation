House of Damnation

Welcome home

Are you ready to be terrified? Originally a backyard haunt, this brand-new trip to hell will mortify you from the moment you set foot inside.

Established in 2012, Revelations Haunted Attractions began as a gathering of family and friends. Today, House of Damnation is something beyond costumes and family fun. This haunted attraction will throw you onto a thrill ride of excitement, as you delve into the world of the dead and depraved.

{% comment %}

Owners Mike Kruzel (designer and builder) and Kris Kruzel (prop designer / makeup and costume effects) built this awe-inspiring haunt with nothing but sweat and passion. We hold the conviction that our haunt will leave you petrified, distressed, and restless.

{% endcomment %}

You will be sweating bullets upon entering. Let's see if you have what it takes to walk through the House of Damnation without succumbing to pure terror and dread.
